# Narrative Guidelines

## Campaign Rhythm

- Maintain one clearly active arc.
- Keep 2 to 4 open loops active at a time.
- Alternate between revelation, pressure, and consequence.
- Let player action change faction posture and location status.

## Escalation

- Escalate by narrowing time, increasing exposure, or raising personal cost.
- Avoid escalating by arbitrary enemy inflation alone.
- Foreshadow later pressure before it lands.

## Encounter Selection

Favor touchpoints that do at least one of these:
- reveal a hidden motive
- force a choice between two costs
- pay off prior setup
- expose the consequence of a previous encounter

Avoid:
- disconnected filler
- duplicate scene purpose back to back
- twists that erase player wins

## State Writing

- Write for fast machine parsing and fast human scanning.
- Keep summaries short and concrete.
- If something changed, name the cause.
- If a loop closes, mark it resolved instead of deleting it.
