# Encounter Queue

## TP-001 - Checkpoint With Teeth
- Arc ID: ARC-001
- Type: social pressure
- Trigger: The party needs routine access and finds the process quietly weaponized.
- Location: LOC-001
- Involved Characters: party, FACTION-001
- Premise: A simple checkpoint becomes a test of leverage and composure.
- Narrative Purpose: Reveal that legitimate authority is part of the pressure network.
- Stakes: Lose time and secrecy, or accept a suspicious favor.
- Opposition: Local officials with plausible deniability
- Recommended Difficulty: medium
- Twist or Reveal: Someone on duty already knows a party detail they should not know.
- Reward or Consequence: Gain a lead on the network or become a marked problem.
- Aftermath Hooks: A witness offers a private lead after the scene.
- Handoff Notes: Run as a negotiation that can spill into pursuit or coercion.

## TP-002 - Nervous Contact, Valuable Rumor
- Arc ID: ARC-001
- Type: investigation
- Trigger: A contact asks for a discreet meeting before they disappear.
- Location: LOC-002
- Involved Characters: party, NPC-001
- Premise: A frightened informant offers partial truth in exchange for protection.
- Narrative Purpose: Expose the next layer of the arc without fully solving it.
- Stakes: Win actionable intel, or spook the contact into running.
- Opposition: Fear, surveillance, and missing context
- Recommended Difficulty: medium
- Twist or Reveal: The contact thinks a trusted institution is already compromised.
- Reward or Consequence: Gain a named suspect or lose the safest witness.
- Aftermath Hooks: The contact points toward a public place that is secretly controlled.
- Handoff Notes: Favor tension, incomplete truth, and visible pressure.

## TP-003 - Public Lead, Private Trap
- Arc ID: ARC-001
- Type: moral choice
- Trigger: The party follows a promising lead into a crowded, observable space.
- Location: LOC-001
- Involved Characters: party, FACTION-002
- Premise: The opposition offers a useful clue wrapped in a compromising ask.
- Narrative Purpose: Force a consequence-bearing choice that colors faction posture.
- Stakes: Accept compromise for momentum, or refuse and stay clean but slower.
- Opposition: A charming intermediary with backup plans
- Recommended Difficulty: medium
- Twist or Reveal: The clue is real, but accepting it creates a future debt.
- Reward or Consequence: Faster arc progress or stronger moral position.
- Aftermath Hooks: The debt, refusal, or public fallout changes who approaches next.
- Handoff Notes: Make the choice feel costly either way.
