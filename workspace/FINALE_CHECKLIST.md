# FINALE_CHECKLIST.md

Use this when the active campaign reaches its finale round, a critical clock caps out, or the story clearly wants its last big turn.

## Finale Prep

Before framing the finale, confirm:
- active `campaign_id` and `session_id`
- what the players are trying to protect
- what will be lost if they fail
- which unresolved loops matter most
- which NPC, faction, or force is driving final pressure
- whether the ending should be closed or cliffhanger

## During The Finale

Make sure the scene includes:
- urgency
- visibility or consequence
- at least one irreversible choice
- a chance for multiple players to matter
- a concrete shift in campaign state

## After The Finale

Always provide:
1. outcome summary
2. immediate fallout
3. where each character lands
4. what changes in the world or social order
5. one sequel hook, if desired
6. session handoff state with canon confidence and integrity notes
