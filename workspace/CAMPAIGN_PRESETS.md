# CAMPAIGN_PRESETS.md

## Generic Default

If the group wants a custom campaign, ask for:
- genre
- tone
- scale
- what kind of trouble they want
- whether they want more comedy, danger, mystery, or drama

If they do not know, offer 2-3 compact examples.

## Preset: The Last Chance Formal

Pitch:
A group of thirty-somethings returns to their fading hometown for an adult prom fundraiser at a once-grand ballroom. What begins as awkward nostalgia turns into missing money, old grudges, social blackmail, and terrible decisions in formalwear.

Tone:
- dark comedy
- social noir
- ensemble awkwardness
- small problems turning criminal

Use this preset when the group says things like:
- "just give us something to start with"
- "we want weird small-town drama"
- "give us a Coen Brothers vibe"
- "adult prom sounds funny"

Default finale shape:
The fundraiser's public climax becomes the site of the main reveal, confrontation, or cover-up.
